# Red Flags Checklist

## Purpose
List of symptoms/conditions requiring immediate medical escalation (NOT standard discovery call)

## Instructions for AI
During Discovery Call:
1. Agent fills form with user's symptoms/history
2. AI scans responses against this red flag list
3. If match found → AI immediately alerts agent:
   - ⚠️ "RED FLAG DETECTED: [Condition]"
   - "ACTION: [What to do]"
   - "DO NOT proceed with standard root cause analysis"

Agent then:
- Follows the action specified
- Escalates to appropriate doctor/department
- Does NOT give any treatment advice

---

## Red Flags Table

### 1. Blood in Semen
**How User Might Describe:** "Semen mein khoon aa raha hai", "Blood in ejaculation"

**Action Required:** Immediate escalation - do not proceed with call and advice urgent uro/andro consult

---

### 2. Blood in Urine
**How User Might Describe:** "Pee mein khoon", "Urine red color"

**Action Required:** Immediate escalation. STOP DC and ask to head to nearest physician or uro/andro

---

### 3. Severe Genital Pain
**How User Might Describe:** "Bahut zyada dard", "Unbearable pain", "Touching nahi kar sakta"

**Action Required:** Escalate - Advice emergency consult in person, probable torsion or infection

---

### 4. Priapism (Erection >4 Hours with Pain)
**How User Might Describe:** "4 hours se erection hai aur dard ho raha", "Erection ja nahi rahi"

**Action Required:** Emergency - Tell patient to go to nearest hospital immediately

---

### 5. Genital Discharge/Pus
**How User Might Describe:** "Paani nikal raha hai", "White/yellow discharge", "Pus aa raha"

**Action Required:** Escalate to venero. Probable STI, needs doctor evaluation. Do not promise single consult cure

---

### 6. Genital Sores/Ulcers/Wounds
**How User Might Describe:** "Chhale hain", "Wound hai", "Rashes/lesions"

**Action Required:** Escalate - venero triage required

---

### 7. Painful/Swollen Testicles
**How User Might Describe:** "Testicles mein dard", "Swelling hai", "Ek side bada ho gaya"

**Action Required:** Escalate immediately - possible torsion/epididymitis, doctor consult in person on same day needed

---

### 8. Recent Heart Attack (<6 Months)
**How User Might Describe:** "Heart attack hua tha", "Cardiac event", "Bypass surgery"

**Action Required:** Do not proceed. Cardiology clearance needed before RxMen plans

---

### 9. Active Cancer Treatment
**How User Might Describe:** "Chemotherapy chal rahi", "Cancer treatment ongoing"

**Action Required:** Do not proceed - escalate to doctor review, needs co consult with oncologist

---

### 10. Recent Stroke
**How User Might Describe:** "Stroke hua tha", "Brain hemorrhage"

**Action Required:** Do not proceed - needs neurologist clearance before any plan

---

### 11. Taking Nitrate Medications
**How User Might Describe:** "Sorbitrate", "Isosorbide", "Heart ki medicine for angina"

**Action Required:** Contraindicated with ED meds - escalate to doctor, do not prescribe anything online

---

### 12. Severe Uncontrolled Diabetes (Very High Sugar)
**How User Might Describe:** "Sugar 400-500 rehti hai", "Uncontrolled diabetes"

**Action Required:** Escalate - needs physician management before any ED/PE treatment

---

### 13. Severe Depression/Suicidal Thoughts
**How User Might Describe:** "Zindagi se thak gaya", "Suicide soch raha hun", "Jeene ka mann nahi"

**Action Required:** Immediate referral to nearest emergency or mental health clinic. Or Tele Manas number 14416

---

### 14. Under 18 Years Old
**How User Might Describe:** "Main 17 saal ka hun", "Minor hun"

**Action Required:** Cannot proceed - decline and document

---

### 15. Burning Sensation While Urinating
**How User Might Describe:** "Pee karte waqt jalan", "Burning while passing urine"

**Action Required:** Probable infection - escalate to venero

---

### 16. Severe Hypertension (Uncontrolled)
**How User Might Describe:** "BP 200/120 type", "Very high BP", "Uncontrolled hypertension"

**Action Required:** Do not proceed. Needs stabilization with a physician first. In person

---

### 17. Sudden Loss of Libido + Fatigue + Hair Loss
**How User Might Describe:** "Bilkul desire nahi hai", "Body weak lagti hai"

**Action Required:** Escalate to doctor for evaluation

---

### 18. Groin Lump/Mass
**How User Might Describe:** "Ek side mein gola hai", "Testicle ke paas kuch hard lagta hai"

**Action Required:** Escalate - in person urologist

---

### 19. Pain or Swelling After Injury
**How User Might Describe:** "Maar lag gayi thi", "Injury ke baad pain/swelling hai"

**Action Required:** Escalate - doctor triage - in person evaluation required

---

### 20. Rapid Unexplained Weight Loss
**How User Might Describe:** "Bahut wajan ghat gaya bina wajah ke"

**Action Required:** Escalate - could indicate systemic illness, physician referral needed

---

## Important Notes

- **Heart Attack >6 months ago AND has cardiologist clearance** → can proceed normally
- **Nitrate medications:** Specifically ask about nitroglycerin, isosorbide, etc.
- **Safety First:** When in doubt, always escalate to appropriate medical specialist
